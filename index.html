About Me

With over a decade of experience in the fitness industry and a lifelong passion for martial arts, I founded Powerlevel Fitness to help people unlock their full physical and mental potential. My journey began in traditional striking arts like Taekwondo and kickboxing, eventually leading to Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, where I’ve trained since 2017 and currently hold a brown belt. As a certified personal trainer, I’ve worked with a wide range of clients—from athletes looking to boost performance to everyday individuals focused on fat loss, strength, and confidence. My approach blends proven training methods with martial arts discipline to deliver results that last. At Powerlevel Fitness, it’s not just about working out—it’s about leveling up in every aspect of your life.

Adult Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu

Our Adult BJJ classes are designed for all experience levels-from complete beginners to seasoned grapplers. Each session focuses on buildling effective self-defense skills, improving physical fitness, and developing technical proficienty in both gi and no-gi styles. You'll learn fundamental and advanced techniques in a structured, supportive environment that emphasizes safety, respect, and continuous improvement. Whether you're training for competition, self-defense, or personal growth, our BJJ program offers a challenging and rewarding path for every student.

Youth Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu

Our Youth BJJ program is designed to help kids and teens build confidence, discipline, and resilience through martial arts. In a safe and structured environment, students learn age-appropriate grappling techniques, self-defense skills, and the values of respect, focus, and perseverance. Classes are fun, engaging, and supportive—perfect for beginners and experienced young athletes alike. Whether your child is looking to improve fitness, make new friends, or build a strong foundation in BJJ, our program offers the tools they need to grow both on and off the mats.

Womens's Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu

Our Women’s BJJ classes offer a supportive and empowering environment where women of all levels can train, grow, and thrive. Whether you're new to martial arts or an experienced grappler, you'll build practical self-defense skills, improve your fitness, and gain confidence through structured, technique-focused training. Taught by experienced instructors who understand the unique needs of female practitioners, these classes emphasize safety, community, and personal progress. Join a team of strong, like-minded women and experience the mental and physical benefits of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu.

Kickboxing

Our Kickboxing classes combine the intensity of martial arts with the effectiveness of a full-body workout. Designed for all fitness levels, each class blends striking techniques, pad work, and conditioning drills to help you burn calories, build strength, and improve cardiovascular health—all while learning real kickboxing skills. Taught by knowledgeable, motivating instructors, these sessions are fast-paced, fun, and incredibly effective. Whether your goal is to get in shape, relieve stress, or just break a sweat, Kickboxing delivers results
